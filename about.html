Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Global Presence

Dream Estates was founded in 1995 by Joshua Ouya with a simple vision: to create a real estate company that prioritizes client needs above all else. Starting from a small office in New York with just three agents, we've grown into an international luxury real estate brand with offices in 12 countries.

Our success stems from our unwavering commitment to integrity, innovation, and exceptional service. We believe that finding or selling a home should be an exciting, stress-free experience, and we've built our reputation on making that belief a reality for our clients.

Client-Centric Approach: Every decision we make is with our clients' best interests in mind. We listen carefully to understand your unique needs and preferences.
